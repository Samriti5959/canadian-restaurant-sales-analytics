# Hakka Restaurant Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Business Overview

Hakka Delight is a restaurant chain operating multiple locations across Canada.

Management wants a centralized analytics solution to monitor sales performance, customer trends, product performance, and profitability.

## Business Problem

Restaurant data is generated daily from multiple locations and contains information about orders, menu items, customers, and revenue.

Management currently lacks a centralized dashboard to monitor performance and identify trends.

## Objectives

- Track total sales and revenue
- Analyze best-selling menu items
- Compare branch performance
- Monitor monthly sales trends
- Identify peak sales hours
- Track customer spending patterns
- Analyze profit margins by menu category

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Sales KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Monthly Revenue Growth

### Product KPIs

- Top Selling Items
- Category Performance
- Food vs Beverage Revenue

### Customer KPIs

- Total Customers
- Repeat Customers
- Average Spend Per Customer

### Operational KPIs

- Branch Performance
- Peak Sales Hours
- Daily Sales Trend
